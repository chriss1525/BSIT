Instructions

Mini ultimatum game that takes 3 players.

Player 1 is given Ksh200 and is asked to send some to Player 2.

Player 2 waits to receive money from Player 1.

Player 3 waits for Player 1 to make an offer to Player 2, and they see the amount before Player 2.

Depending on how Player 3 feels about the amount, they can choose to:

  • Punish: If Player 3 punishes Player 1, both Player 1 and Player 2 do not get any money.
  • Not Punish: If Player 3 does not punish Player 1, Player 1 gets their amount - the amount sent to Player 2, and Player 2 gets the amount sent to them by Player 1.
